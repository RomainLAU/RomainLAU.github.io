Who am I

Hi ! I'm Romain LAURENT, aka Le Roi Adibou, a french student in web development at Epitech Digital in Paris. I started coding 3 years ago, in 2019, by making little programs in Python. In 2020 I discovered the web development -- with HTML, CSS and PHP -- and at this moment, I knew that was what I wanted to do later. I love to start new projects where I don't know how I will do it, but I just know I'll do it. I like to work during night to fully be in peace, when nobody can disturb me, and there only are my project and me.

Random Shapes and Colors Show the project

At college I had an art class, and for a project I decided to use Python (with turtle module) to build a shape generator, you can decide of the shape (square, circle, triangle or parallelepiped), and the number of turns (the number of shapes drawn), and it will choose randomly a position, a color and a size for each shape.
