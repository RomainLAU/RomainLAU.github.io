Who am I

Hi ! I'm Romain LAURENT, a french student in web development at Epitech Digital in Paris. I started coding 3 years ago, in 2019, by making little programs in Python. In 2020 I discovered the web development -- with HTML, CSS and PHP -- and at this moment, I knew that was what I wanted to do later. I love to start new projects where I don't know how I will do it, but I just know I'll do it. I like to work during night to fully be in peace, when nobody can disturb me, and there only are my project and me.
