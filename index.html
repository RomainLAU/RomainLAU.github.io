Who am I

Hi ! I'm Romain LAURENT, a french student in web development at Epitech Digital in Paris. I started coding in 2019, by making little programs in Python. In 2020 I discovered the web development -- with HTML, CSS and PHP -- and at this moment, I knew that was what I wanted to do later. I love JavaScript, TypeScript, ReactJS and VueJS... I think we can say I'm a frontend developer ! I like to work during night to fully be in peace, when nobody can disturb me, and there only are my projects and me. I love colors (as you can see on my hair), and that's why I had an art class in high school. And I think it explains pretty well why, even if I'm a developer, I'm really interested in design and UX/UI. Currently, my dream would be to get a website displayed on the Awwwards website...

Random Shapes and Colors Show the project

In highschool I had an art class, and for a project I decided to use Python (with turtle module) to build a shape generator, you can decide of the shape (square, circle, triangle or parallelepiped), and the number of turns (the number of shapes drawn), and it will choose randomly a position, a color and a size for each shape.
